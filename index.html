PopProjects Bio

PopProjects is a small but creative web development group that specializes in building retro, fun, and simple websites. We’re not a huge team, but we love creating unique, lighthearted web experiences using the basics of web development: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Our goal is to bring a touch of nostalgia to the web, while keeping things simple, entertaining, and easy to use

At PopProjects, we focus on crafting websites that are fun, quirky, and easy to navigate. We’re not about high-tech complexity – we love creating websites that remind you of the early days of the internet, with playful designs and straightforward functionality

If you’re looking for a retro, simple, and fun website that’s easy to use and brings a smile to your face, PopProjects is the team for you. We may not be the biggest group, but we love what we do, and we’re all about making the web a little more playful
